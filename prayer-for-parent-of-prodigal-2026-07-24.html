A Prayer for the Parent of a Prodigal

For parents aching over a child who has walked away — holding on to God when love feels unanswered

Hope

▶ Listen in English

Lord, I did not expect it to hurt like this. My child is gone — not dead, but gone from me in the ways that matter most — and I carry this grief in places no one else can see. I replay everything, wondering what I missed or what I should have done differently, and some nights the weight of it crushes me. I know the story of the prodigal son, and I cling to the father who ran. Help me to be that kind of waiting — not passive, not resentful, but alive with hope that is rooted in you rather than in their choices. Let me release my grip on an outcome I cannot control and trust that you love my child even more fiercely than I do. Show me how to keep the light on without burning myself out in the waiting. I believe you are already at work in their life, in ways I cannot see. Hold us both. Amen.

Reflection

In Luke 15, the waiting father does not merely accept his son's return — he sees him 'while he was still a long way off' and runs. That kind of watching is itself an act of faith. If you are a parent in this pain, you are not alone, and your love for your child is a faint echo of the love God holds for both of you.

A Prayer for the Parent of a Prodigal

▶ Escuchar en Español

Señor, no esperaba que doliera así. Mi hijo se ha ido — no muerto, sino alejado de mí en las formas que más importan — y cargo este dolor en lugares que nadie más puede ver. Repaso todo, preguntándome qué perdí o qué debí haber hecho diferente, y algunas noches el peso de ello me aplasta. Conozco la historia del hijo pródigo, y me aferro al padre que corrió. Ayúdame a ser ese tipo de espera — no pasiva, no resentida, sino viva con una esperanza que está arraigada en ti y no en las decisiones de ellos. Déjame soltar mi control sobre un resultado que no puedo controlar y confiar en que tú amas a mi hijo aún más fieramente que yo. Muéstrame cómo mantener la luz encendida sin agotar mis fuerzas en la espera. Creo que ya estás obrando en su vida, de maneras que no puedo ver. Sostenlos a los dos. Amén.
