A Prayer for Anxiety

Peace

Lord, my mind won’t slow down and my chest feels tight with worry I can’t name. I know You said not to be anxious — but I am. So I’m coming to You not with peace, but asking for it. Take the weight I’ve been carrying like it’s mine to fix. Remind me that You are already in what I’m afraid of. Let me breathe. Let me trust. Let me rest in You. Amen.

A Prayer for Waiting

Patience

Father, I’ve been waiting a long time. For the door to open. For the answer to come. For things to change. I don’t understand Your timing and I won’t pretend I do. But I choose to trust that You are working in the silence. That this season has a purpose I can’t see yet. Hold my hope steady. Keep me from bitterness. Let me wait well. Amen.
