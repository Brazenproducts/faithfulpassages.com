Where Can I Go

Based on Psalm 139:7-12 · Contemporary Worship Ballad

Based on Psalm 139:7-12 · Contemporary Worship Ballad

▶ Listen in English

Verse 1: I've run to the edge of everything I know Stood where the shadows fall and the cold winds blow Thought if I hid myself you'd never find this place But I looked up and found you there, looking at my face Chorus: Where can I go from your Spirit Where can I run from your love If I make my bed in the darkness You are there — you are there In the morning you find me In the deep you hold me still There is nowhere, there is nowhere I can go beyond your will Verse 2: I've tried to disappear inside my shame and fear Thought I had finally gone too far for you to hear But even in the breaking, even in the fall Your hand was underneath me, you were holding through it all Chorus: Where can I go from your Spirit Where can I run from your love If I make my bed in the darkness You are there — you are there In the morning you find me In the deep you hold me still There is nowhere, there is nowhere I can go beyond your will Bridge: Even the darkness is light to you Even my silence, you hear it too Even the places I dare not speak You see the whole of me, broken and weak And still you stay — still you stay Still you stay
