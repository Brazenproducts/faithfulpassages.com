When You Don't Know What to Pray

Honest words for anxiety, grief, waiting, fear, shame, and hope. For the moments faith feels hardest.

For Peace

Anxiety & Fear

A Prayer for Anxiety

Peace

Lord, my mind won't slow down and my chest feels tight with worry I can't name. I know You said not to be anxious — but I am. So I'm coming to You not with peace, but asking for it. Take the weight I've been carrying like it's mine to fix. Remind me that You are already in what I'm afraid of. Let me breathe. Let me trust. Let me rest in You. Amen.

A Prayer for Fear

Courage

Father, I am afraid. Not of something small — of something that feels like it could undo me. I've read that You have not given me a spirit of fear. But right now it doesn't feel like a choice. So I'm asking You to do what I cannot do myself: Replace this fear with something steady. Not bravado. Just quiet trust. Remind me You've already been to where I'm headed. Go before me again. Amen.

For Hard Seasons

Grief, Waiting & Loss

A Prayer for Grief

Comfort

God, I am grieving and I don't know how long this is supposed to last. I've heard You are close to the brokenhearted. I need that to be true right now. I'm not asking You to fix it or explain it. Just — be here. In the silence. In the ache. Hold me in a way I can feel even when I can't feel anything. Help me believe this loss doesn't have the final word. Amen.

A Prayer for Waiting

Patience

Father, I've been waiting a long time. For the door to open. For the answer to come. For things to change. I don't understand Your timing and I won't pretend I do. But I choose to trust that You are working in the silence. That this season has a purpose I can't see yet. Hold my hope steady. Keep me from bitterness. Let me wait well. Amen.

For the Soul

Shame, Burnout & Healing

A Prayer for Shame

Grace

Lord, I've done something I'm not proud of. Or I've believed something about myself that I'm not sure You'd agree with. Either way, shame is sitting heavy on me right now. I know You say there is no condemnation for those in Christ — but it doesn't always feel that way from the inside. Remind me who I am in You. Not what I've done. Not what was done to me. Speak over the shame with something truer. Amen.

A Prayer for Burnout

Rest

God, I have nothing left. I've been running on empty for longer than I should have, and I'm done pretending otherwise. I thought rest was something I had to earn. You said it was a gift. Help me receive it without guilt. Help me stop measuring my worth by my output. Let me be still — really still — in You. Restore me from the inside out. Amen.

A Prayer for Healing

Healing

Father, I'm asking for healing. I don't know exactly how You will answer — in full, in part, or not the way I'm expecting. But I believe You are a God who heals, and I'm bringing this to You. Be near to my body, my mind, my spirit — wherever the wound is deepest. Give wisdom to those who are helping. Give patience to me as I wait. And whatever happens, let me know You are in it. Amen.

For Daily Life

Work, Relationships & Purpose

A Prayer for Work & Money Stress

Provision

Lord, the financial pressure is real and I am tired of worrying about it. I've done what I can. I've tried to be responsible. And still I'm staring at numbers that don't add up. You said You would provide for my needs. I'm trusting You to mean that. Help me to work with my full effort and let go of the rest. Provide in ways I haven't thought of yet. And keep me from the fear that money is something You can't handle. Amen.

A Prayer for Wisdom

Guidance

God, I don't know what to do. I've thought about it. I've asked people I trust. I've prayed about it before. And I still don't have clarity. You promised to give wisdom generously to anyone who asks — so I'm asking. Not a feeling, not a sign — just clear understanding of what's right. Lead my thinking. Settle my restlessness. And when the answer comes, help me trust it enough to move. Amen.

A Prayer for Purpose

Calling

Father, I want my life to mean something. Not fame. Not legacy. Just — something real. I feel like I was made for more than this routine, but I don't know what more looks like. Show me where I'm needed. What I'm built for. Give me small, faithful steps toward something only You could build through me. And remind me that purpose doesn't always look important from the outside. Let me be useful in the way You made me. Amen.
